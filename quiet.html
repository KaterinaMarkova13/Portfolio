    Designing for Social Impact
    and Inclusion

    Hidden poverty often goes unnoticed, leaving many without access to essential resources or support. Quiet Eindhoven aims to bridge this gap by creating meaningful connections between people who can give and those who need help the most. The challenge was to understand how technology could strengthen this mission — ensuring fair distribution of opportunities, improving engagement among members, and supporting volunteers in managing their tasks more efficiently, all while fostering dignity and inclusion within the community.

    Solution

    Designing for Fairness and Connection

    The solution was divided into two applications — one for sponsors and volunteers to manage offers and coordinate activities, and another for community members to view and request “geluksmomentjes.” Both are designed with clarity and inclusivity in mind, making it easier for everyone to connect, communicate, and contribute to Quiet Eindhoven’s mission of reducing hidden poverty.
